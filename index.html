ABOUT ME

Graphic Designer

Hi there! I am Somto Chukura, a designer with a passion for the art of creative discovery and the magic of visual storytelling. Good design, to me, not only looks good but also makes you feel something and makes you curious and want to engage with it. My design philosophy is rooted in thinking outside the box, merging playfulness and creative imagination with laser-like attention to creating distinctive and effective work.

Skilled in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and After Effects, I thoroughly enjoy getting dynamic visuals underway. It's always my goal to create designs that are not only graphically interesting, but also connect on a level further, resonating positively and lingering in the memory.

I'm always refining my process, finding new sources of inspiration, and love taking observations of everyday life and breaking them down into compelling visual experiences. If you're searching for a designer who can bring a distinct voice and passion for innovative, emotionally intelligent design, I'd love to hear from you.
